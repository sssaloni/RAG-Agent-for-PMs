Q1 2025 RETROSPECTIVE PATTERNS (SUMMARY)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Recurring themes across all 6 sprints:

1. Cross-team dependencies are the #1 source of unplanned work. Surfaced in 4 of 6 sprints. The dependency matrix approach in S05 was the first effective mitigation. Should be standard practice.

2. Security reviews landing mid-sprint caused disruption in 3 consecutive sprints (S01-S03) before the pre-sprint review process fixed it. Process change worked — zero mid-sprint security blockers in S04-S06.

3. Sprint scope creep driven by optimism bias. Team consistently accepts 10-15% more points than sustainable velocity. PM ownership of this problem acknowledged.

4. Documentation is a persistent gap. API docs and test coverage have both been flagged in 3+ sprints. Need structural enforcement (acceptance criteria, Definition of Done) rather than reminders.

5. Team velocity stabilised at 88% average after the S01 backlog refinement. Q4 2024 average was 74%. The investment in process hygiene is paying off.
